Frequently Asked Questions

Are my financial or personal PDF files completely secure?

Yes. All file processing happens entirely locally within your browser using JavaScript. We offer a high-level secure environment where your files are never uploaded to any external cloud servers.

Is there a limit to how many PDFs I can process?

No. Our tool is designed for unrestricted use. You can merge, split, and edit unlimited documents instantly without any premium paywalls or hidden limits.

Will the quality of my PDF decrease after merging or splitting?

Not at all. We use lossless processing algorithms. Your output files retain the exact same DPI, resolution, and text clarity as your original documents.
