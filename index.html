How It Works

Powerful PDF editing in three simple steps — no software to install.

  1. 1. Choose your tool & upload

    Select the PDF operation you need from the tool grid above. Click the tool card to open the workspace, then drag and drop your PDF files directly onto the upload area — or click to browse. You can upload multiple files at once for batch operations like merging or splitting.

  2. 2. Adjust settings

    Each tool presents only the controls you actually need. Reorder pages with drag-and-drop, set a compression level, choose a page range to extract, or pick a rotation angle. Everything is live-previewed so you can see exactly what the output will look like before committing.

  3. 3. Download your file

    Click the action button and your processed PDF is ready to download in seconds. The file is generated entirely inside your browser using the PDF-lib library, so there is no round-trip to any server. Once you close the tab, nothing is retained.

Why choose PDF Tool Expert?

Unlike most online PDF services, PDF Tool Expert processes every file locally in your browser. Your documents are never uploaded to a remote server, which means sensitive contracts, medical records, financial statements, and personal files stay exactly where they belong — on your own device. There are no accounts to create, no subscription fees to manage, and no hidden usage limits.

Our tools are built on PDF-lib, an open-source JavaScript library that handles complex PDF manipulation natively, giving you professional-grade results without plugins or desktop software. All major browsers on both desktop and mobile are supported, so you can rotate a scan on your phone or merge a report on your work laptop with equal ease.

We take privacy seriously. Our Privacy Policy is written in plain language, and our technical architecture is designed so that even we cannot access your documents. If you need to process confidential files regularly, PDF Tool Expert is one of the few free tools where that is genuinely safe to do. Fast, free, and truly private — that is what we built this for.
